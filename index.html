Who is Freshworks STS Software Academy for?

Freshworks STS Software Academy (FSSA) is a programme exclusively designed for aspirants of a career in
technology, who do not have the means to afford formal education after high school

Zero Cost Programme

Yes, you heard that right! You don't have to
pay any fee throughout the course. Instead,
you will receive a monthly stipend to aid your
learning for the entire duration of the
programme

Industry Aligned Learning

Your education will incorporate elements from
dynamically changing industry trends & the
latest-in-technology practices to maintain
perfect synergy between learning and
application

Career Launchpad

Upon successful completion of the course,
you will be given an opportunity to work at
Freshworks and other partner organisations
recruiting from FSSA
